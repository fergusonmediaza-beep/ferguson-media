Our Journey

How Ferguson Media Was Built.

The Idea
A nation winning with no record of it

SA's great innovators, entrepreneurs and teachers were going unrecorded. Ferguson Media was built to close that gap — with real journalistic standards, not just feel-good content.

The Model
Solutions journalism, powered by brand investment

Not funded by ad clicks or outrage. Funded by brands that understand the business value of being written into South Africa's story.

The Ecosystem
Three pillars, one engine

Good News Portal anchors our digital presence. Community Media Hub shares into townships. Creative Hub produces everything to broadcast standard.

The Vision
The permanent record

An archive so complete and widely shared that the South Africa narrative changes — not just in the media, but in the minds of the people who live here.
